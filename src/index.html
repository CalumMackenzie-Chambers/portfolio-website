About me

Path of a Software Developer

As a seasoned Web Developer and Software Engineer, I offer bespoke web design and development solutions to a global clientele, operating from my base in Surrey, England. I can provide a localised advantage to clients in Greater London and the surrounding areas, however, my commitment is to empower your business, wherever it may be, with a global standard of excellence.

I have a broad range of experience in the Software Engineering space, from Web Development to B2B multi-tenant applications and a multitude of internal tools. At Infuse IT, I contributed to refining the low/no-code testing tool useMango™ by leading agile, ISTQB-certified teams to comprehensively test and enhance the product.

In my ongoing role at Pharmica, I've navigated beyond traditional web development to engineer comprehensive solutions for our complex operational needs. My work includes developing and optimising significant internal tools, which play a crucial role in Pharmica's day-to-day operations. These intricate, large-scale projects require a keen eye for both performance and compliance to successfully cater to the needs of our 1.2 million+ user base.

In every project, my goal is to understand and meet your unique needs, crafting customised digital experiences that combine visual appeal with robust functionality. My passion for continuous learning and adapting to the latest technologies means I'm always ready to tackle your ideas head-on and bring them to life.

FAQs

Essential Questions Answered

How much does a custom website cost?

The cost of a custom website varies based on complexity, features, and specific client needs. You could have a simple website such as this one for as little as £150 but depending on the complexity websites can cost tens of thousands of pounds to build. For detailed pricing, please refer to our pricing section. Alternatively if you already have a plan for your website you can fill out the contact form below and I will get back to you with further details.

What is your payment policy for web development projects?

For small and medium-sized projects, I generally require a 50% upfront payment, with the remaining 50% due upon completion of the project. Custom projects will require further discussion to find something that works for you or your business.

How long does it take to build a website?

For a simple site like the portfolio that you are currently reading, I can get your site up and running within 24 hours if required. Generally though for simple sites you are looking at 1 week to 1 month, but complete custom solutions could take many more months. With a full list of requirements by email I can provide more accurate information.

Do you provide support and maintenance after the website is launched?

Yes, all plans will offer free bug fixes for life providing the code hasn't been modified by external sources. In addition the advanced plan will come with one year of premium support for free and after that it will be charged at an agreed monthly rate. Since you own all of your code you are also free to maintain the repository internally or outsource to another developer.

Can you help with website hosting and domain setup?

Absolutely! I can assist in setting up hosting and configuring your domain as part of my web development services. I also can host the website for you on small / medium sized projects. For large projects there would be an in depth discussion on the hosting strategy.

Are your websites mobile-friendly and responsive?

Yes, all websites I develop are fully responsive and mobile-friendly, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices and screen sizes. I extensivly test on a wide range of devices to ensure a smooth experience and also use Browser Stack to ensure compatability with older devices where it is needed.

Do you follow SEO best practices when building a website?

Definitely. I incorporate SEO best practices in my web development process, including optimizing site speed, mobile responsiveness, and using semantic HTML.

What technologies do you specialize in for web development?

I specialize in a variety of modern web technologies including TailwindCSS, Go, DotNet, and others. I choose the technology stack based on the project requirements.

Can you integrate e-commerce functionalities into a website?

Yes, I have extensive experience in integrating a range of e-commerce functionalities into websites. For an example you can check out Pharmica where I am a part of building out features for their ecommerce platform

How do you ensure website security and data protection?

Ensuring the security and protection of data on the websites I develop is a top priority. Being in the EU I also ensure that everything that I develop will meet GDPR regulations
